Tubbataha

Measuring 99,600 hectares in size, the Tubbataha Reef National Marine Park is made up of 2 atolls rising in the middle of the Sulu Sea.

Learn more about Tubbataha

Apo Reef

The location and isolation of Apo Reef serves as filter for the marine stocks of the Sulu and South China Seas. The diverse marine life with more than 385 species of fish make every dive unique and unforgetable!

Apo Reef & Coron

Apo Reef & Coron is characterized by two season: dry from December to May and wet from June to November with average humidity of 75% almost year round. Changes in weather condition doesn’t affect diving visibility.

Learn more about Apo Reef & Coron
