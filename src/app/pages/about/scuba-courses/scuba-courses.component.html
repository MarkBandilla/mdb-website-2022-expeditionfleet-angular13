PADI Specialty Courses

All PADI specialty courses must be booked in advance and are subject to availability of Instructor and conditions of dive sites. Please contact us in advance for requirements and bookings.

All specialty courses include the PADI material and Certification Card. The course fee will be collected onboard in US $ or PHP.

Our Scuba Courses

Enriched Air Diver

Stay down longer and get back the water sooner!
Scuba diving with enriched air nitrox gives you more no decompression dive time. This means more time underwater, especially on repetitive scuba dives on a liveaboard.
We offer the PADI Enriched Air Nitrox specialty course on all our trips and you can enjoy the advantages of diving with nitrox immediately. After completing the course, students get FREE Nitrox on the whole trip!

US $ 250

Enriched Air Diver

Explore the wonders of the deep!
Deep dives require planning and discipline in order to safely enjoy all the fantastic things that can be found. In this course you learn techniques for safe diving in the deeper ranges and go to the maximum of recreational diving.
Most dive insurances don’t cover the expenses caused by dive accidents at deeper depths. This course might help you extending these limits. Please check your insurance!

Multilevel Diver

Maximize your dive time so you can explore more!
In this course, you learn how to plan dives that extend your bottom time by crediting you for slower nitrogen absorption when you ascend to a shallower depth. You’ll also get to see the various types of multilevel dive calculators (including dive computers), as well as learn about multilevel dive planning, organization, procedures, techniques, and potential problems.

US $ 200

Drift Diver

This course will blow you away!
In the PADI drift diver course you will learn how to enjoy ocean currents by “going with the flow,” staying with your dive partner, communicating with the dive boat and knowing where you are the whole time.
