Package Inclusions

  • Aircon accommodation on board
  • Full board meals
  • No-decompression diving (5 dives a day including night dive)
  • Use of tanks, weights,weight belt, boat and services of a Divemaster

Package Exclusions

  • Land transfers to from Manila Airport to Batangas and v.v. for the transition trips
  • Domestic air tickets: Manila/Puerto Princesa/Manila for the regular trips
  • Alcoholic drinks and soda
  • Equipment rental (complete gear US $ 15.00/diving day)
  • Nitrox fills
  • Dive and Travel Insurance
  • Other incidental expenses

Additional Fees

  • Apo Reef Conservation Fee US $ 38/person (only for Transition Trip)
  • Tubbataha Conservation Fees US $ 78/person

How To Get There

Guest going to Tubbataha have to take domestic flights out of Manila to Puerto Princesa and back. Philippine Airlines and Air Philippines fly this route. The embarkation and disembarkation point for the transition trips is Anilao Outrigger Resort in Anilao, Batangas.
